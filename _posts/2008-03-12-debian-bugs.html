I couldn't find a sorted list of number of bugs per package, so I extracted one myself with this command:


wget -O /dev/stdout http://bugs.debian.org/cgi-bin/pkgindex.cgi?indexon=pkg|grep pkgreport.cgi | sed 's:.*pkg=::'| sed 's:">.*has::'|sed 's: bug.*::'| awk '{print $2 " " $1}'| sort -nr


I know, it's not very romantic code... but it works.

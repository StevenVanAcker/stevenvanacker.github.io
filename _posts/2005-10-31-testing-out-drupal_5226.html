--- layout: post title: Testing out Drupal date: '2005-10-31T17:58:00.002+01:00' author: Steven Van Acker tags: modified_time: '2008-01-16T09:15:37.994+01:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-5513234137363262204.post-945522204992271621 blogger_orig_url: http://www.singularity.be/2005/10/testing-out-drupal_5226.html --- I've decided to look at Drupal for my new website. It's not that I'm tired of coding PHP and looking for interesting CSS constructs, but I believe the Drupal people do a better job at it.
I've found that installing the software is really easy and works out of the box.
The configuration and tuning is something that will require more work. I'm still reading the documentation (Why isn't there a PDF version of this ? Then I could print it for easier reading)

I'm excited about all the contributed Drupal modules and I'm planning to deploy Drupal in a lot more places if this experiment turns out to be a success.

A couple of things to note:

First, don't believe anything coming from a hardwarestore. Well, maybe not anything, but still it's a good idea to doublecheck it.
As you can see on this picture [G2:11592], the width of my planks is not 36 but 35mm.

Also, it doesn't look like any of the planks I bought was straight. I didn't notice it at the store because all 10 were tied well together. So keep that in mind...

The drivers used by the OPVP device are actually dynamically loaded libraries implementing this API:
ftp://ftp.pwg.org/pub/pwg/fsg/vector/pdapi-spec-1.0rc1.pdf

I would be fun to try to implement this and hook it up to my simulator.

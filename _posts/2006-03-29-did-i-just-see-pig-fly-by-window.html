I couldn't believe it. It really read that VLD and MR had formed a majority on the Picanol law which would force companies to publish the salaries of the topmanagers.

Of course it all blew up in the afternoon. VLD announced that it wanted to change the law that was agreed on, so Vlaams Belang pulled out.

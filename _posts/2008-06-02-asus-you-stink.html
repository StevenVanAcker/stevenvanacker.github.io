A colleague of mine is planning to get an Eee PC from ASUS because it's cheap and very portable. If it gets stolen while you're traveling, you don't lose a lot of money.

I'm planning to go on holiday soon too, and wanted to check out the specs of the Eee PC since I've heard nothing but good about it.

Both links I found on the site I linked to above are fully flash:

http://eeepc.asus.com/global/guide.htm

http://event.asus.com/eeepc/microsites/en/index.htm

So, what's that about ASUS ? What possible advantage could you get from having the useful part of your site in flash ? The only part of that site that interests me, is the part that shows the specs. And when I got there, I was unable to zoom into the small text shown.

Big downer for ASUS.

I was tempted to write programs in assembler, but after finding this article:
http://www.micahcarrick.com/v2/content/view/14/21/
I think I will just use C to do it. When I have a better grasp of how the PIC functions (I tried reading the datasheet, but it's real dry stuff), I can always do things in assembler.
